Common questions

Answered honestly.

Do both people need to participate?
For the Compatibility report, yes — both partners fill their profiles separately. For Horoscope, both birth details are needed. For Verification, consent is required from the person being verified.
How is my data protected?
All data is stored on Indian servers and is fully compliant with the DPDP Act 2023. Verification data is deleted within 90 days. We share nothing with third parties.
Can I order reports one at a time?
Yes. You can start with any single lens and add more later. The bundle prices are available when you are ready to combine two or three reports.
Who writes the reports?
Every report is written by qualified professional psychologists including Certified Prepare-Enrich professionals.
What if I am not satisfied with the report?
If you feel a report is incomplete or unclear, we will arrange a follow-up call with the expert who prepared it at no additional charge. Please see our refund policy for full details.
Is there a self-verification option?
Yes. You can commission a verification of yourself and share the resulting Verified badge with the other family. Trust offered, not demanded.
