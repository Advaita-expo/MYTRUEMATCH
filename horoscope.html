What a real reading covers

I
First Aspect

Ashtakoot Guna Milan

The traditional thirty-six points. Varna. Vashya. Tara. Yoni. Graha Maitri. Gana. Bhakoot. Nadi. Each explained in writing — not whispered as a number.

II
Second Aspect

Mangal Dosha

Present, absent, severe, or cancelled. A senior astrologer assesses each chart individually. No blanket verdicts. No unnecessary alarm.

III
Third Aspect

Nadi Dosha

When present, one of the most considered concerns in traditional Jyotish. We discuss severity and the remedies experienced astrologers consider effective.

IV
Fourth Aspect

Dasha Compatibility

The Mahadasha and Antardasha periods you will run together. A marriage that begins well can enter difficult years. You deserve to know in advance.

V
Fifth Aspect

Navamsa Chart

The D9 chart — the chart of marriage in Vedic astrology. Analysed for both partners. What the birth chart alone cannot reveal.

VI
Sixth Aspect

Remedies

Where challenges are found, specific guidance. Puja recommendations. Gemstone suggestions with honest caveats. Never expensive. Never theatrical.
