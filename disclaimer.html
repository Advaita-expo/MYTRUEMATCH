All reports are prepared in good faith by qualified professionals. However, the information contained in our reports is provided for informational and advisory purposes only. Section 02

Compatibility Reports

First Lens
Compatibility Report

Compatibility assessments are professional psychometric analyses based on the information provided by both parties. They reflect the expert opinion of a trained psychologist and are intended to facilitate informed conversation — not to predict, prescribe, or determine the outcome of a relationship.

A high compatibility score does not guarantee a successful marriage. A low score does not indicate an unsuitable match. Compatibility reports identify patterns and areas for attention; what couples do with that information remains entirely their own choice.

The accuracy of a Compatibility report depends on the honesty and completeness of the information provided by both parties. MyTrueMatch takes no responsibility for reports that are based on inaccurate, incomplete, or misleading submissions.

Section 03

Verification Reports

Second Lens
Verification Report

Verification reports are based on information available from public records, consented sources, and legitimate verification channels at the time of verification. They reflect the state of available information as of the date of the report and are not a guarantee of completeness or perpetual accuracy.

Our verification specialists exercise professional diligence, but they cannot access sealed court records, non-public financial information, or information withheld or falsified by the subject. A report that returns no adverse findings should not be interpreted as a comprehensive clearance.

  • Verification reports do not constitute legal due diligence or a legal background check as defined under Indian law.
  • They should not be used as the sole basis for any legal, financial, or employment decision.
  • Results are valid as of the date of report delivery. Circumstances may change after verification is complete.
  • MyTrueMatch is not a licensed investigation agency and does not hold a Private Security Agency licence under the PSARA Act, 2005.
Section 04

Horoscope Reports

Third Lens
Horoscope Report

Horoscope readings are traditional astrological analyses prepared by experienced Jyotish practitioners. They represent the professional interpretation of planetary positions and classical astrological texts, and are provided for cultural, spiritual, and informational guidance.

Astrology is a traditional belief system with deep roots in Indian culture. MyTrueMatch neither endorses nor dismisses astrological belief. We provide access to experienced practitioners for those who value this perspective as part of their decision-making process.

  • Astrological readings are not scientific predictions. They should not be treated as fact or used as the sole basis for any decision.
  • The presence or absence of any Dosha, favourable or unfavourable planetary period, or astrological concern is the considered opinion of the astrologer — not a deterministic outcome.
  • MyTrueMatch does not guarantee the accuracy of any astrological interpretation.
  • Remedies suggested in a Horoscope report are traditional in nature. Clients undertake any remedy at their own discretion and risk.
